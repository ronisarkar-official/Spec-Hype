Borderlands 4 System Requirements

As anticipation builds for the next installment in the beloved Borderlands franchise, many gamers are wondering if their PC setup can handle the upcoming game. While Gearbox Software has yet to release official system requirements, we have made an informed prediction based on recent trends in gaming hardware and software optimization. In this guide, we will provide a detailed forecast of the expected Borderlands 4 PC requirements and help you determine whether your system is ready for the action-packed adventure ahead.

Predicted Minimum System Requirements for Borderlands 4

For players looking to experience Borderlands 4 in 4K resolution with maximum settings and ray tracing, these are the expected requirements:

  • OS: Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6500 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • RAM: 8GB
  • Graphics Card: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB / AMD Radeon RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 100GB NVMe SSD
  • Internet: High-speed broadband for seamless multiplayer and updates

This setup should ensure ultra-high quality graphics with stable 120+ FPS in 4K.

Performance Tips for Borderlands 4

If you’re concerned about whether your PC can handle Borderlands 4, here are some ways to optimize your setup:

  1. Upgrade Your GPU: Consider at least an RTX 2060 or RX 6600.
  2. Increase Your RAM: Aim for 16GB or more for smoother performance.
  3. Use an SSD: Faster load times and better game performance.
  4. Adjust In-Game Settings: Lower shadow quality, anti-aliasing, and textures.
  5. Update Your Drivers: Always install the latest GPU drivers.

Will Borderlands 4 Support Ray Tracing and DLSS?

It’s highly likely Borderlands 4 will support NVIDIA DLSS 3.0 and AMD FSR 3 for enhanced visuals and performance.

  • Ray Tracing: Realistic lighting, shadows, and reflections.
  • DLSS/FSR: AI upscaling boosts FPS with minimal quality loss.

Multiplayer and Online Requirements

As a co-op-driven looter-shooter, Borderlands 4 will likely require a stable internet connection for:

  • Online Co-Op: Up to 4-player seamless multiplayer.
  • Cross-Platform Play: Likely support across PC, Xbox, and PlayStation.
  • Dedicated Servers: For smoother, more reliable online play.

Recommended minimum internet speed: 10 Mbps

Will Your PC Run Borderlands 4?

Quick checklist to assess your system readiness:

  • GPU: GTX 1060 / RX 580 or better
  • CPU: i5-6500 / Ryzen 3 1200 or newer
  • Storage: Preferably an SSD
  • RAM: At least 8GB (16GB recommended)

If you meet the recommended or ultra specs, you’re ready for a smooth and immersive experience!

It's important to get your system ready whether you want to get 60+ FPS at high levels or just have stable performance. As the release date gets closer, keep an eye out for Gearbox Software's official system requirements. This way, you can make sure your setup is ready.
