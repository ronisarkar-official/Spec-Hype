Cricket 24 PC System Requirements (2025)

Discover everything you need to know before installing Cricket 24 on your PC in 2025. From minimum to recommended specs, performance tips, and the best streaming setups—we’ve got you covered!

What’s New in Cricket 24 for PC Gamers?

Cricket 24 isn’t just another update—it's a full-blown evolution. With lifelike player animations, realistic stadium lighting, upgraded crowd audio, and official tournament licenses, the game feels like watching a live broadcast. Career mode has seen major upgrades too. But all this realism comes at a cost—it needs more powerful hardware than its predecessors.

Minimum PC Requirements (2025)

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • CPU: Intel Core i3-2100 / AMD FX-6300
  • RAM: 8 GB DDR3
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 (2 GB VRAM)
  • Storage: 60 GB (HDD or entry-level SSD)

This setup will let you run the game at low settings and 720p resolution. Don’t expect high frame rates—30–40 FPS is typical.

Recommended PC Requirements (2025)

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-6600K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • RAM: 16 GB DDR4
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1660 Ti / AMD RX 580
  • Storage: 60 GB SSD (NVMe preferred)

With this build, you’ll enjoy smooth 60–75 FPS at 1080p with high settings. SSD storage will cut load times and prevent stuttering during replays or transitions.

Top 10 Performance Tips for Cricket 24

  1. Play at 1080p or 900p for smoother gameplay.
  2. Set Texture Quality to “Medium” for 4 GB GPUs.
  3. Use FXAA instead of MSAA to reduce GPU load.
  4. Turn off motion blur and depth of field for clarity.
  5. Always install the game on an SSD.
  6. Update your graphics drivers regularly.
  7. Enable “Game Mode” in Windows settings.
  8. Close Chrome, Spotify, or heavy apps while playing.
  9. Use MSI Afterburner for safe GPU overclocking.
  10. Monitor FPS and temps with on-screen display tools.

Ideal Build for Competitive Play & Streaming

  • CPU: Intel Core i5-12600K / AMD Ryzen 7 5800X
  • GPU: RTX 3060 / RX 6700 XT
  • RAM: 16–32 GB DDR4 (3200 MHz)
  • Storage: NVMe SSD (Samsung 970 Evo, WD SN850)
  • Extras: Capture card (Elgato), Dual Monitors, OBS Studio

This setup handles full-match streaming, commentary overlays, and background apps without dropping a frame. Perfect for Twitch and YouTube creators.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can Cricket 24 run on laptops?
A: Yes, if it has a dedicated GPU like GTX 1650 or higher and 8 GB RAM.
Q2: Is the game AMD-friendly?
A: Mostly yes, but NVIDIA GPUs often handle shaders and effects better.
Q3: Is 4K gaming possible?
A: Yes, but only with a GPU like RTX 3070 or RX 6800 XT. Expect around 60 FPS.
Q4: I have good specs, but the game stutters. Why?
A: Check for thermal throttling, background apps, or enable V-Sync to stabilize FPS.
