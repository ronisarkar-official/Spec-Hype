System Requirements Overview

Minimum Requirements

  • RAM: 8 GB
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-4460
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 750ti 4G / AMD Radeon RX550
  • Storage: 50 GB
  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit

Recommended Specifications

  • RAM: 16 GB
  • CPU: Intel Core i7-7700
  • Graphics: GTX 1060 6G / RX 580 / Intel ARC A380
  • Storage: 50 GB
  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit

Can Your PC Run Once Human?

Check if your PC meets or exceeds the minimum requirements. Let’s analyze each component to see how it affects performance.

Detailed System Requirements Breakdown

  • RAM: 8 GB is minimum, but 16 GB is ideal for smooth visuals and multitasking.
  • CPU: The i5-4460 is basic; the i7-7700 is optimal for data processing and frame stability.
  • Graphics Card: Key for rendering visuals. A modern GPU improves realism and FPS.
  • Storage: Ensure at least 50 GB is free for installation and updates.
  • Operating System: Windows 10 64-bit is required. Stay updated for compatibility.

Performance Expectations

Frame rates depend on hardware quality. Consider these key performance factors:

  • Average FPS: Dependent on settings and specs.
  • CPU & GPU: Crucial for rendering and data processing.
  • RAM: Affects load time, stability, and responsiveness.

Optimizing Your PC

If you fall short of the recommended setup, consider upgrading RAM or your graphics card. These two components have the biggest impact on gaming performance.

Troubleshooting Performance Issues

Use an FPS monitor tool to identify bottlenecks. Adjust in-game settings like resolution, shadows, and anti-aliasing for smoother gameplay.

Conclusion

To enjoy Once Human at its best, your system should meet or exceed the recommended requirements. Upgrade strategically, tweak performance settings, and you’ll be ready for an immersive experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my PC only meets the minimum requirements?

You can run the game, but performance may be limited. Lower settings may be required.

How can I check my PC’s specs?

Use Windows System Info or tools like Speccy, CPU-Z, or DXDiag.

Do I need a specific GPU?

Your GPU must meet or exceed the listed requirements for a playable experience.

What role does RAM play?

More RAM allows for smoother asset loading and better multitasking during gameplay.

Can I play Once Human on Mac or Linux?

No. The game is only supported on Windows 10 64-bit.
