Subnautica 2 System Requirements Explained

Whether you're upgrading your PC or just curious, it’s important to know what hardware you'll need. Here’s a complete breakdown of the minimum and recommended specs for smooth gameplay.

Minimum System Requirements

  • Operating System: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 5 2600 or Intel Core i5-8400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1660 or AMD RX 5500 XT
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: At least 20 GB of free space
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection

Recommended System Requirements

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or newer (64-bit)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 7 7700X or Intel Core i7-13700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: AMD RX 5600 XT or NVIDIA RTX 2060
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 20 GB SSD preferred
  • Internet: High-speed broadband

Why These Specs Matter

Not sure why these numbers are important? Let’s break it down in simple terms:

  • CPU (Processor): This is your system's brain. It processes everything happening in the game. A better CPU means smoother gameplay, even during intense underwater scenes.
  • RAM: Think of RAM as your PC's short-term memory. More RAM helps the game load faster and prevents lag.
  • GPU (Graphics Card): Subnautica 2 is visually stunning. A powerful GPU ensures all those deep-sea creatures and glowing coral reefs look amazing without frame drops.
  • Storage: Loading the game from an SSD instead of an HDD will cut down waiting times dramatically.

How to Boost Performance

Want the best experience? Here are some quick tips to get the most out of your current setup:

  1. Keep your drivers updated: Regularly update your graphics drivers. New updates often improve performance in recent games.
  2. Lower graphics settings if needed:
    • Reduce screen resolution for smoother performance.
    • Turn off or lower high-demand effects like shadows, reflections, and anti-aliasing.
  3. Monitor your system: Use tools like MSI Afterburner to watch temperatures and performance to avoid overheating or hardware throttling.

FAQs

  1. Can I play Subnautica 2 with 8 GB RAM?
    Yes, 8 GB is the minimum requirement. However, for a smoother experience, especially with multitasking, 16 GB is better.
  2. Will Subnautica 2 release on consoles?
    Right now, it’s confirmed for PC. Console news might come later, so stay tuned.
  3. Is an internet connection required?
    Yes, especially for multiplayer features. You’ll want a stable broadband connection.
  4. How can I make the game run better on a low-end PC?
    Lower the graphics settings, close background apps, and update your GPU drivers regularly.
  5. When exactly is Subnautica 2 coming out?
    Early Access is planned for 2025. The full release date hasn’t been announced yet.
