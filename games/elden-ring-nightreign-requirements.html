Can You Run Elden Ring: Nightreign? Full System Requirements Guide

Elden Ring: Nightreign is an exciting standalone story set in the iconic Elden Ring universe. Scheduled to launch in 2025, now’s the perfect time to check if your PC is ready for this epic adventure. Let's break down everything you need — from system requirements to gameplay features and tips.

What Is Elden Ring: Nightreign?

Developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco, Nightreign is a co-op action RPG set in a dynamically generated version of Limgrave. You’ll team up with others, tackle missions across three in-game days, and face off against a final boss. It’s all about teamwork and survival.

Minimum System Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit or newer
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-7400 / AMD Ryzen 3 3200g
  • RAM: 12 GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1060 / AMD Radeon RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 60 GB of free space

Recommended Specs for Smooth Gameplay

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit or newer
  • CPU: Intel Core i7-8700K / AMD Ryzen 5 3600X
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1070 / AMD Radeon RX Vega 56
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 60 GB of free space

Storage Tips

Although the game needs 60 GB of space, installing it on an SSD is highly recommended. It boosts load times and overall performance. Running low on space? Upgrade your SSD to keep room for updates and mods.

Graphics & Performance

A GTX 1070 or equivalent should deliver smooth gameplay on high settings. For ultra settings or 4K resolutions, consider investing in a stronger GPU to future-proof your rig.

Get Your PC Ready

  • Keep Windows Updated: Avoid bugs and compatibility issues.
  • Upgrade If Needed: Don’t let outdated RAM or GPU slow you down.
  • Free Up Disk Space: Remove unused files and apps.
  • Update Drivers: Get the latest graphics and system drivers.

Key Gameplay Features

  • Procedurally Generated Limgrave: Every run is different.
  • 3-Player Co-op Mode: Teamwork is critical to survive.
  • Dynamic Challenges: Shrinking maps and evolving threats keep things intense.

Tips for Better Play

  • Talk with Your Team: Communication is key in co-op missions.
  • Use Resources Wisely: Stock up and save for the final fight.
  • Adapt Quickly: Each world is different — be ready to change your strategy.

Conclusion

Elden Ring: Nightreign is shaping up to be a must-play experience for fans and newcomers alike. By preparing your system now and brushing up on what to expect, you’ll be all set to take on the darkness when it arrives.
