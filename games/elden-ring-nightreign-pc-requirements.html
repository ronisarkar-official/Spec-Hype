Elden Ring Nightreign System Requirements

Prepare your rig for the standalone Elden Ring: Nightreign, launching in 2025. This guide covers one-page overview, detailed specs, performance tips, networking, mods, and a pre-launch checklist.

Contents

    Overview & Gameplay

    FromSoftware and Bandai Namco bring you Nightreign, a co-op action RPG set in a procedurally generated Limgrave. Team up with two allies over three in-game days of shifting missions. Adaptive AI, shrinking zones, and environmental hazards demand strategy and synergy.

    Release Details

    • Release Window: 2025 (Exact date TBA)
    • Platforms: Windows PC, PS5, Xbox Series X/S
    • Distribution: Steam, GOG, Microsoft Store

    Elden Ring Nightreign Minimum Requirements

    • OS: Win 10 64-bit (1809+) / Win 11
    • CPU: Intel i5-7400 (3.0 GHz) / Ryzen 3 3200G
    • RAM: 12 GB DDR4 @ 2666 MHz
    • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1060 (3 GB) / AMD RX 580 (4 GB)
    • DX: DirectX 12
    • Storage: 60 GB HDD (SSD recommended)
    • Network: 5 Mbps up/down (co-op)

    Elden Ring Nightreign Recommended Requirements

    • OS: Win 10/11 latest build
    • CPU: Intel i7-8700K / Ryzen 5 3600X
    • RAM: 16 GB DDR4 @ 3200 MHz
    • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1070 / AMD Vega 56
    • DX: DirectX 12+
    • Storage: 60 GB NVMe SSD
    • Network: 10 Mbps up/down

    High-End Build (4K, 60 FPS)

    • CPU: Intel i9-12900K / Ryzen 7 7800X3D
    • RAM: 32 GB DDR5 @ 5200 MHz
    • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 3080 Ti / AMD RX 7900 XT
    • Storage: 100 GB NVMe SSD
    • Cooling: Custom water-cooling or AIO

    Storage & Loading

    Install on NVMe SSD for sub-10 second load times. Enable Windows Game Mode, disable unnecessary startup apps, and reserve at least 20 GB for updates and mods.

    Graphics Tuning & Features

    • Resolution Scaling: Dynamic resolution to maintain 60 FPS.
    • V-Sync & Frame Cap: Align with monitor refresh to eliminate tearing.
    • Ray Tracing: DX12-only; best on RTX 20/30/40-series.
    • DLSS / FSR: Use DLSS 3 or FSR 3 for boosted frame rates.

    Networking & Co-op

    • Use wired Ethernet to minimize latency.
    • Open UDP 3074, TCP 27015–27050 for hosting.
    • Enable router QoS to prioritize gaming traffic.

    Overclocking & Cooling

    A mild CPU overclock (+10–15%) can boost performance. Set GPU fan curve: start ramp at 50 °C, max at 80 °C. Keep components under 85 °C via MSI Afterburner or HWMonitor.

    Community Mods

    • Visual: High-res textures for sharper vistas.
    • Quality-of-Life: HUD customizers, save-state tools.
    • Custom Campaigns: Fan-made maps on Nexus Mods.

    Pre-Launch Checklist

    • Update Windows & GPU drivers within 48 hours of release.
    • Verify game files via your launcher.
    • Back up existing Elden Ring saves if using mods.
    • Watch official Nightreign livestream for last-minute tips.

    12. FAQs

    No — Nightreign sessions are fully procedural and separate.
    Q2: Can I play solo?
    Yes, but co-op yields the full experience.
    Q3: Any pre-order bonuses?
    None announced. Check official channels for updates.
